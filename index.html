Dynamic Chart Generator

Instructions:

1. Upload a CSV file containing your data.

2. Select the X and Y axes from the dropdowns.

3. Enter a title for your chart (optional).

4. Click "Generate Chart" to create the dynamic chart.

Example CSV Format:

                X, Y
                1, 30
                2, 29
                3, 31
                4, 16
                5, 24
                ...
